Hanabi Hyuga

Hanabi is the younger sister of Hinata. She plays an important role in The Last: Naruto the Movie. Her father is Hiashi Hyuga and is auntie to Hinata's children, Boruto and Himawari. Her cousin is Neji Hyuga. Naruto Uzumaki is her brother-in-law.

Hanabi Hyuga is a kunoichi of Konohagakure and the heiress of the Hyūga Clan. She is the daughter of Hiashi Hyūga and the younger sister of Hinata Uzumaki, as well as the aunt of Boruto and Himawari Uzumaki. She is currently Team 15's leader.
